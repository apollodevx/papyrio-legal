1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and any optional profile information you provide.

Social Media Account Information

Papyrio allows you to create an account and log in through third-party social media services including:

  • Google
  • Apple ID

If you choose to register through a social media service, we may collect personal data associated with your social media account, such as your name, email address, profile picture, and contact list associated with that account.

User Content

We collect any photos (including portraits and face images), story prompts, or other materials you upload or submit when using the app. This content is used to create personalized stories and artwork based on your submissions.

Use of Face Data

Papyrio values your privacy and is committed to handling personal information, including "face data," responsibly and transparently. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, "face data" refers only to the photographs that users voluntarily upload to the Papyrio app in order to generate illustrated characters. We do not collect, access, or use any biometric identifiers (such as Face ID templates, facial geometry, or landmark data from Apple's ARKit).

Why We Collect Face Data

We request user-uploaded photos containing faces solely for the purpose of generating customized illustrated characters in your personalized storybooks. These images are also used to enable our before-and-after feature, which lets you see the original photo side by side with the illustrated character. Face data is never used for authentication, profiling, marketing, or advertising purposes.

How Long We Store Face Data

Face data is retained only as long as the associated book exists in your account, up to a maximum of 12 months. This retention period is necessary so that you can continue to view the before-and-after feature of your books. After 12 months, or if you delete your book earlier, the uploaded photos are permanently deleted from our systems. We do not store face data indefinitely.

Sharing of Face Data

  • Papyrio does not sell or share face data with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.
  • In order to provide our services, face data is processed by our contracted third-party cloud service providers (for example, AI model hosting and secure cloud storage).

Why We Share Face Data with Third Parties

We share face data with these providers only to perform the image-to-illustration transformation service and to securely store your book. These providers act strictly as data processors on our behalf and do not use the data for their own purposes.

Third-Party Storage and Retention

Where third-party providers temporarily store face data to enable processing and hosting, they are contractually obligated to:

  • Use the data only for the limited purpose of providing the Papyrio service.
  • Protect the data using industry-standard security measures.
  • Automatically delete the data in accordance with the same retention period we follow (up to 12 months, or sooner if the book is deleted).

We do not work with any third-party providers that permanently retain or repurpose user face data.

Payment Information

All payments are securely processed through Stripe. Papyrio does not store your credit card numbers or billing details.

Usage Data

We collect data about how you interact with the app, including:

  • Which features you use
  • How many tokens you spend
  • Which screens you visit
  • Time and date of your visits
  • Time spent on pages
  • Unique device identifiers

Device & Technical Information

We may collect comprehensive technical information including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address (Internet Protocol address)
  • Browser type and version
  • Mobile device unique ID
  • Mobile operating system
  • Type of mobile Internet browser
  • App crash logs and diagnostic data
  • Unique device identifiers

Device Permissions and Camera Access

Camera Permission Notice: This app requires camera access for core functionality.

With your prior permission, we may access:

  • Camera: We request camera permission to allow you to take photos directly within the app for creating stories and content. This permission is only used when you actively choose to take a photo through the app interface.
  • Photo Library: We access your photo library when you choose to select existing photos for your stories and content creation.
  • Contacts: Information from your device's contacts list (optional, for sharing features).

Important: Camera permission is required for core app functionality. We do not access your camera in the background or without your explicit action. Photos taken or selected are only processed as part of your content creation and are subject to the data handling practices described in this policy.

You can enable or disable access to these permissions at any time through your device settings, though disabling camera access may limit certain app features.
