Delete Your Papyrio Account

To request deletion of your Papyrio account and associated data:

  1. Email us at support@papyrio.com with "Delete Account" in the subject
  2. Include the email address associated with your account
  3. We'll confirm deletion within 7 business days

What gets deleted:

  • Your account information
  • Created books and content
  • Personal preferences

What we keep:

  • Basic transaction records (required by law for 7 years)

Process completes within 30 days of request.
