Contact Support

We're here to help! For the fastest assistance, please email us at support@papyrio.com with "Help Account" in the subject line.

When contacting support, please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • A detailed description of the issue you're experiencing
  • Screenshots or error messages (if applicable)
  • Steps you've already tried to resolve the issue

Technical Support

Experiencing technical difficulties with the app? We can help troubleshoot:

  • App crashes or performance issues
  • Feature not working as expected
  • Login or authentication problems
  • Content generation issues

Please include your device type, operating system version, and app version when reporting technical issues.

General Inquiries

For general questions about Papyrio features, pricing, or how to use the app, feel free to reach out. We're always happy to help you get the most out of your Papyrio experience.

Support Email: support@papyrio.com

Typical Response Time: 24-48 hours

Support Hours: Monday - Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM PST
